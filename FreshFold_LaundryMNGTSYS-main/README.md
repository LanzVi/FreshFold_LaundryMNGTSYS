# FreshFold_LaundryMNGTSYS
Final Project for subject: IntProg32 - Full Stack Authentication System Deployment

Web-Based Laundry Wash Managment System for: Customers, Staff, and Admin (Owner)

To address the following issues:
    1. reducing paper waste of physical receipts upon customer's Ordered Service Transaction of the Laundry shop.
    2. Customers experience friction when trying to get quick, accurate service quotes and booking flexible drop-off times that fit their busy schedules.
    3. Staff Experience operational inefficiency due to unmanaged customer arrival spikes
    4. Lack of proper permanent records for profit tracking for the owners leading to gaps in estimating the overall revenue within specified time.
